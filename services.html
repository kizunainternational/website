Our Services

Full-Circle Support From Your First Question to Your First Day in Japan

Practical support for institutions, documents, visa steps, and life after arrival.

Free Educational Counselling

We help you decide if Japan fits your career, finances, and goals.

  • Japan's education system and realistic expectations
  • Institution, course, and city options
  • Tuition, accommodation, living costs, and part-time income possibilities
  • Application timelines and visa requirements
  • Clear answers for students and parents

In-House Japanese Language Classes

Learn N5 to N3 Japanese in Kathmandu for class, work, and daily life.

  • Led by a Japan-trained language department head
  • Visual and audio practice materials
  • Real communication preparation beyond textbooks
  • Course fee: NPR 5,000 / 5 months

Institutional Matching & Admission Processing

We match students with suitable recognized institutions.

  • Formal agent agreements with 28+ Japanese institutions
  • Application preparation and submission
  • Document collection and verification
  • Translation and notarization guidance
  • Document formatting for institutions and the Japanese Embassy

Visa Guidance & Application Support

After COE approval, we guide your Japan student visa process.

  • COE and visa document review
  • Embassy process preparation
  • Final checklist before submission
  • College fees paid only after receiving COE

Pre-Departure Support

We prepare you for Japan beyond the paperwork.

  • Culture, etiquette, and daily life orientation
  • Accommodation and transportation guidance
  • Part-time work rules and legal rights
  • Communication with your institution after arrival
  • Fee transfer guidance after COE approval

Post-Arrival & Ongoing Support

We stay connected through our partner institutions.

  • Enrollment, attendance, and progress follow-up
  • Phone support for challenges after arrival
  • Airport pickup coordination with partner schools
  • Refund coordination in rare visa denial cases

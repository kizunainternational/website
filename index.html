Introduction

Honest Guidance Built on a Lasting Bond

Kizuna (絆) means "bond" in Japanese. We build that bond between Nepali students and quality Japanese institutions.

Since 2074 B.S., we have guided students from first counselling to airport pickup with transparent, practical support.

We also run in-house N5 to N3 Japanese classes and work directly with immigration-recognized institutions.

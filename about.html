About Us

About Kizuna International Education Pvt. Ltd.

Nepal's dedicated Japan study consultancy, built on honest guidance, language preparation, and trusted institutional relationships.

Our Story

A Focused Pathway to Japan

Kizuna International Education Pvt. Ltd. was established in 2018 A.D. (2074 B.S.) and is registered with Nepal's Company Registrar and Ministry of Social Development.

Kizuna means "bond" in Japanese. We build that bond with students, parents, and trusted institutions across Japan.

Our team combines Japan experience with 20+ years of Japanese language instruction, giving students practical guidance from Nepal to Japan.

Mission & Values

What Guides Every Student Decision

Our Mission

To prepare Nepali students for Japan with honest counselling, strong language training, and reliable institutional links.

Honesty First

We tell students what they need to hear, not only what they want to hear.

Student-Centered

Every recommendation considers the student's career, finances, readiness, and wellbeing.

Quality Over Quantity

We select motivated students and recommend reputable institutions that match their goals.

Long-Term Support

Our relationship with students does not end at the airport; we continue supporting them after arrival.

Recognized Work

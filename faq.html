Frequently Asked Questions

Short answers for students and parents planning for Japan.

Is Kizuna registered and recognized by the government?

Yes. Kizuna is registered in Nepal and approved by the Ministry of Social Development.

Do I need to know Japanese before I apply?

No. We teach N5 to N3 Japanese in-house before departure.

When do I pay the college fees?

Fees are transferred only after COE approval from Japan Immigration.

What happens if my visa is denied?

We coordinate the refund with the institution. Standard application fees may be deducted.

Can I work while studying in Japan?

Yes. Students can work up to 28 hours per week during academic terms.

Is Japan safe?

Yes. Japan is clean, organized, and known for low crime and reliable transport.

What if I have problems after arriving in Japan?

You can contact Kizuna, and partner schools also support students in Japan.

How do I get started?

Call, WhatsApp, or visit us in New Baneshwor. Initial counselling is free.
